Everything you need

Powerful scheduling features

ShiftSync provides all the tools you need to efficiently manage employee schedules and keep your team organized.

Calendar Dashboard
View your entire team's schedule at a glance with intuitive week and month calendar views. Easily navigate between dates and see who's working when.
Shift Management
Create, edit, and publish shifts with just a few clicks. Assign employees to shifts, set departments, and notify your team instantly when schedules are ready.
Day-Off Requests
Employees can submit time-off requests directly through the app. Managers receive instant notifications and can approve or deny with a single click.
Real-Time Notifications
Stay informed with instant in-app and email notifications. Get alerts when shifts are assigned, schedules are published, or requests are processed.
